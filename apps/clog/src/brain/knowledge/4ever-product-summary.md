# 4ever Product Summary

`4ever.ai` is a private platform for preserving family memories and legacy. It is for families and close friends, not for public social posting.

The product centers on:

- photos, stories, and recipes
- personal profiles for family members
- a visual family tree
- timelines connected through shared moments
- private collaboration and contribution controls
- `Eve`, an AI assistant that helps users recall and expand memories

The emotional and trust stakes are high. Users are storing meaningful family history, so reliability, privacy, and clarity matter more than novelty.
