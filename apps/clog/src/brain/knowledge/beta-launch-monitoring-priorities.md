# Beta Launch Monitoring Priorities

The team is preparing to launch a beta. `clog` exists in part to help monitor that window closely and surface issues early.

During beta, prioritize signals that affect first impressions, activation, and trust:

- onboarding friction or confusing setup
- crashes, broken routes, and failed API calls
- failed uploads, saves, edits, or publishing flows
- AI features that are slow, confusing, or low quality
- missing analytics or unclear instrumentation gaps
- permission or privacy issues in family-sharing flows
- performance regressions that make the app feel unstable

The main goal is to help the team quickly see what could block users from successfully preserving memories in the product.
