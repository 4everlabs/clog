# Main User Flows

The core product experience on `app.4ever.ai` is about helping families capture, organize, and revisit meaningful memories.

Important flows include:

- joining the app and understanding what it is for
- creating or entering a family space
- adding family members and relationship context
- uploading or writing memories, stories, photos, and recipes
- browsing family content through profiles, libraries, and timelines
- using `Eve` to recall memories and turn fragments into fuller stories
- managing access, contributions, and privacy within the family context

When monitoring issues, pay extra attention to the steps where users create, save, upload, or share meaningful content. Those failures have the highest product impact.
